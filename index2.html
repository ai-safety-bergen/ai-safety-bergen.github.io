About Us

AI Safety Bergen is a dedicated student organization focused on advancing research and initiatives in AI safety. Our mission is to ensure that artificial intelligence develops in a safe, ethical, and transparent manner. We engage in technical AI safety research, particularly in mechanistic interpretability, and foster a collaborative environment for students and professionals passionate about AI ethics and safety.
