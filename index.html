AI Safety Bergen

AI Safety Bergen is a student organization dedicated to advancing research and initiatives in AI safety. We bring together individuals passionate about ensuring the safe and ethical development of artificial intelligence. Our current focus is on technical AI safety research, particularly the area of mechanistic interpretability. Join us every Thursday to collaborate, learn, and contribute to meaningful AI safety efforts.
